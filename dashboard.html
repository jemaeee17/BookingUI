About Us

Assafbooking is an innovative and user-friendly web and mobile application designed to simplify the booking process for a wide range of services and experiences. With a focus on convenience and accessibility, the platform offers users the ability to reserve and book unique spaces such as capsule rooms, capsule hotels, capsule offices, and capsule shops, providing a modern and efficient solution for accommodation, work, and retail needs.

In addition to these practical offerings, Assafbooking also provides a diverse array of entertainment options tailored to various interests. From street shows and theater performances to art studio capsules, gym capsules, museum shows, drawing and sculpture capsules, game pad capsules, and cultural activities, users can easily explore and engage in a multitude of leisure experiences.

Based in Dubai, Assafbooking caters to different cities across the United Arab Emirates, ensuring that users have access to a broad selection of services and activities. By connecting customers with a variety of service providers in the region, Assafbooking serves as a one-stop-shop for all booking and reservation requirements, streamlining the process and enhancing the user experience.

Our Services

Assafbooking provides rental and booking services for various capsule-based amenities such as capsule shops, offices, gyms, hotels, and rooms. These services are designed to offer compact, functional spaces that cater to diverse needs, ranging from work and retail to fitness and accommodation.

The booking rates range from $20 USD to $60 USD, depending on the specific service and location. Assafbooking's user-friendly web and mobile application covers various cities across the United Arab Emirates, including Dubai, ensuring users can easily access and enjoy these innovative capsule-based experiences.
